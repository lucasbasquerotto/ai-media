Chapter 4: Weather Control Core

Kai and Vera slipped through the maintenance corridors beneath the city. The hum of servers filled the air as they reached the weather control core. Vera projected encrypted data onto the wall. "These protocols are locked down. Someone doesn’t want us here."

A shadowy figure stepped from the darkness — Dr. Lin, a city official. "You’re meddling in things you don’t understand," Lin warned, voice low. "Leave this alone, for your own good."
