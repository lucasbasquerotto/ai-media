Chapter 1: The Glitch in the Sky

Kai stood on the rooftop, gazing at the endless neon-lit skyline. The artificial clouds above flickered for a moment — just a glitch, or something more? Vera’s voice echoed in his earpiece, calm but alert. “Kai, anomaly detected in the weather system. Do you wish to investigate?”

Kai’s curiosity sparked. “Let’s check it out, Vera. Something’s not right tonight.”
